HISTORY

On May 1, 1904, the businessmen signed a cooperation agreement. This is the date of foundation of the company «Rolls-Roys».

1907 Rolls-Royce Silver Ghost.

In 1919, the Rolls-Royce became the most prestigious car in the world again. However, the fifth generation of this model was replaced not by the ultra-exclusive Phantom IV, but by the archaic Rolls-Royce Silver Wraith, developed back in 1938. If the Phantom IV of 1950-1956 was created exclusively for the British royal family and leaders of foreign countries, then the Phantom V was designed for the richest representatives of other estates: from presidents and governors to movie stars and bandstand. From 1959 to 1968 , the company sold 516 copies of the Phantom V. Among the buyers were Queen Elizabeth II, Governor of Hong Kong, Shah of Iran Mohammad Reza Pahlavi, King Olaf V of Norway, President of Yugoslavia Josip Broz Tito and musician John Lennon.

The beginning of the story

The history of this brand is the story of a surprisingly successful union of two businessmen, Charles Rolls and Henry Royce. One of them was a rich aristocrat, and the other grew up in poverty and only went to school for a year, but together they created a car that became an absolute symbol of success. The name of the Rolls-Royce company consists of two surnames. These are the surnames of the founding fathers of the company - Charles Rolls and Henry Royce. The history of their brand is a classic case of a successful business union of an investor and an inventor.

An English company, a division of BMW AG, specializing in the production of luxury cars under the Rolls-Royce brand.
